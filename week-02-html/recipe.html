Amok (Coconut fish curry)

Amok trey is Cambodia's national dish

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a pan.
  2. Add the eggs and stir until cooked.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add the cooked rice and mix everything together.
  5. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  6. Stir well and serve hot.

Nutrition Information

Nutrient Amount
Calories 350
Protein 12g
Fat 10g
